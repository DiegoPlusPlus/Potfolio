Kapor Center IT

SMASH Rising, a 5-10 week micro-internship + workplace exposure program, where interns solve real-time challenges faced by SMASH Rising partners. Interns work in teams with a dedicated Project Manager to deepen their critical thinking and presentation skills. Using the SMASH Rising design thinking and rapid prototyping curriculum, interns create high-impact solutions for partners.

Hack The Hood

At Hack the Hood our goal is to create a community of care that supports early-career Black, Latinx, AAPI, and Indigenous talent in their tech career journeys. One of the most powerful ways we do this is by matching our young people with mentors they can identify with and who can be a champion, coach, and trusted industry connection as they pursue their career journeys.

SMASH Berkeley

Completed a three-year intensive, residential college-prep program, empowering students of color to deepen their talents and pursue STEM careers. I gained problem-solving and professional development skills through weekly networking events with professionals pursuing STEM and college-level coursework.
